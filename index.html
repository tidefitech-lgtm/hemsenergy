Technical Capabilities

Choose the Protection You Need

Whatever your subsea challenge, Hem Energy Limited delivers with speed, precision and safety. Get your quote online and deploy with confidence.

Pipeline Inspection

High-resolution video and sonar inspection of subsea pipelines and risers to detect corrosion and anomalies before they become critical.

Subsea Repair

Hot-stab connections, clamp fitting, valve actuation and emergency repair operations executed by our work-class ROV systems.

Offshore Structure

Biofouling removal, anode inspection, structural integrity surveys and cathodic protection checks on offshore installations.

Survey & Mapping

Multibeam sonar bathymetry, USBL positioning and HD video surveys providing centimetre-accurate subsea site mapping.

Cable Intervention

Power cable and umbilical inspection, burial depth verification, bend-stiffener checks and emergency cable burial.

Rapid Deployment

Emergency mobilisation within 24 hours for blowout response, lost-equipment recovery and critical offshore incidents.

Installation Support

ROV-assisted template placement, foundation monitoring and subsea equipment installation in ultra-deep water.

Scientific Sampling

Sediment sampling, environmental baseline surveys, faunal analysis and deep-water ecosystem monitoring services.
